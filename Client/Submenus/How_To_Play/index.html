How to play

1 / 5

Welcome to Twoodle Jump! Get ready for an epic multiplayer game where you'll jump higher than your opponent to claim victory!

Starting the game is super easy. Just enter a number as a passcode to save your progress, give Player 1 and Player 2 a name, and press start. If you need to continue where you left off, just press the “load game” button and enter your passcode and press start to get right back into the game.

2 / 5

LEVELS

With three incredible levels to explore, you'll have a blast conquering each one. The first level is a peaceful meadow where you'll jump on grassy platforms and use power-ups to mess with your rival. The second level is a bit trickier, an ice level where you'll slide around on slippery platforms while trying to jump higher than your opponent. The third and toughest level is a spooky haunted castle where you'll jump on moving platforms while dodging ghosts!

3 / 5

POWER-UPS

But wait, there's more! You'll come across power-ups throughout each level that give you an advantage or disadvantage. Super jump will launch your character higher than normal, super speed will make you run faster than ever before, and reverse controls will flip the direction of your opponent's controls. Watch out for the ghosts in the final level, though - they'll try to spook you and freeze you for a few seconds!

CONTROLS

The controls are super easy to master, so you'll be jumping in no time. Player 1 will use the arrow keys while Player 2 will use the "wasd" keys. Use left and right arrows or "a" and "d" to move left or right, and the up arrow or "w" key to jump. Be careful not to fall off the platforms, or your opponent might get ahead and win the game!

5 / 5

OBJECTIVE

To win the game, be the first player to win two rounds by jumping higher than your opponent on the screen. It's a race to the top, so make every jump count! Get ready to jump, dodge, and outmaneuver your opponent to be crowned the Twoodle Jump champion. Good luck and have a blast playing!

Press Main Menu in the bottom left corner to go back!
